Hydrodynamic Cavitation
Explained

At the core of every HydroCav solution lies patented hydrodynamic cavitation—an elegant physical process that generates microscopic bubbles. When these bubbles collapse, they release intense heat and pressure waves, destroying pathogens and precipitating contaminants without chemicals.

  • Instant neutralization of Legionella, Cryptosporidium, E. coli, and Pseudomonas.
  • Removal of iron, lead, arsenic, and other heavy metals down to < 5 µg/L.
  • Up to 75% reduction in chlorine and biocide consumption.
