FlowForge Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

FlowForge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 AI Context Data

FlowForge collects and processes AI context data to provide flow state tracking and productivity insights. This includes:

  • Session timing and duration information
  • Flow state indicators and metrics
  • Project and task organization data
  • Productivity analytics and patterns

Storage: All AI context data is stored locally on your device by default. Optional cloud sync uses end-to-end encryption.

2.2 Flow State Information

To track and optimize your flow state, we collect:

  • Session start and end times
  • Flow score calculations
  • Context switch events
  • Interruption patterns

2.3 Coding Session Analytics

FlowForge analyzes your coding sessions to provide insights:

  • Session quality metrics
  • Productivity heat maps
  • Weekly and monthly trends
  • Habit tracking data

2.4 Photos and Media

With your permission, FlowForge may access:

  • Camera for flow state documentation photos
  • Photo library for saving documentation

Note: Photos are stored locally and never uploaded without explicit user action.

2.5 App Activity Data

We collect app usage information including:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide flow state tracking and monitoring
  • Calculate productivity metrics and insights
  • Send context health alerts and notifications
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure data security and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage. Your code and context data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

4.2 Data Encryption

FlowForge uses industry-standard encryption:

  • At Rest: AES-256 encryption for local data
  • In Transit: TLS 1.3 for all network communications
  • Cloud Sync: End-to-end encryption when enabled

4.3 Database Security

Local databases are encrypted and protected by device-level security features including biometric authentication when available.

5. Third-Party AI Services

FlowForge may integrate with third-party AI services for enhanced features. When using these integrations:

  • Data sharing is always opt-in
  • Only necessary context is shared
  • Third-party processing follows their privacy policies
  • You can revoke access at any time

Current Third-Party Services:

  • Analytics providers (for app performance)
  • Cloud storage providers (when sync is enabled)
  • Notification services (for push notifications)

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or acquisitions (with notice to you)

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion (right to be forgotten)
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

7.2 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising rights

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete the app or clear data
  • Cloud Sync Data: Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized within 90 days
  • Support Data: Deleted after resolution plus 12 months

9. Children's Privacy

FlowForge is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

If you're using FlowForge outside your country of residence, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Privacy Shield frameworks (where applicable)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities

11. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications for:

  • Context health alerts
  • Flow state transitions
  • Productivity reminders
  • Daily check-in prompts

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

12. Cookies and Tracking

FlowForge uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential: Required for app functionality
  • Analytics: To understand app usage (can be disabled in settings)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use advertising trackers

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if you've provided one)
  • App store update notes

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Website: https://flowforge.app/privacy
  • Support: https://flowforge.app/support

Email: dpo@flowforge.app

16. Additional Information

16.1 Open Source Components

FlowForge uses open source components. Their licenses and privacy policies are available in-app under Settings > About > Open Source Licenses.

16.2 Security Incidents

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

16.3 Your Control

You maintain full control over your data:

  • Export your data at any time (Settings > Data > Export)
  • Delete all data (Settings > Data > Delete All Data)
  • Disable cloud sync (Settings > Sync > Disable)
  • Opt-out of analytics (Settings > Privacy > Analytics)

This privacy policy is effective as of November 2025 and applies to all users of FlowForge mobile applications and services.
